More Info
Consultation Required
All bookings require a consultation beforehand. Please fill out the contact form before proceeding with any booking.

Deposit & Payment
• 50% non-refundable deposit to secure booking
• Final balance due at end of shoot
• Payment via Cash, Zelle, Apple Pay, Venmo or Cashapp
• Proofs sent after full payment
Delivery Times
• Standard: 3-9 business days
• 24-hour rush: +$50 upcharge
• Turnaround starts after you make selects
Late Policy
• 15+ mins late: +$20 fee
• Overtime: $1.75 per minute
• 20+ mins late: session canceled, no refund
Rescheduling
• Weather-related rescheduling allowed
• Rain forecast or temp below 65°F
• Cancellations require new deposit to rebook
Session Details
• Maximum 2 guests allowed per session
• All raw images provided without watermark
• Professional editing on all delivered images
